WHO AM I

Neil Patel

I'm a New York Times bestselling author and was recognized as a top 100 entrepreneur under the age of 30 by President Obama and a top 100 entrepreneur under the age of 35 by the United Nations.

I've helped companies like Amazon, NBC, GM, HP and Viacom grow their revenue through digital marketing.

My Story

I started my first website when I was 16 years old. I didn't know anything about marketing, but I learned quickly. I made a lot of mistakes along the way, but those mistakes taught me valuable lessons.

Over the years, I've built multiple successful companies and helped thousands of businesses grow their online presence. My mission is to help entrepreneurs and marketers succeed in the digital world.

Today, I run NP Digital, a global performance marketing agency that helps brands grow through data-driven strategies and creative solutions.
